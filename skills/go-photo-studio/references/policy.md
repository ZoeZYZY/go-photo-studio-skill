# Policy Boundaries

## Allowed
- Portrait enhancement and style transfer that preserves identity
- Professional, resume, and generic ID-style photos
- Realism-oriented retouching constraints

## Not Allowed
- Celebrity face replacement or impersonation
- Non-consensual intimate or exploitative image generation
- Fraudulent document forgery guidance
- Requests to alter age/ethnicity in deceptive ways

## Safety Defaults
- Preserve facial geometry and age cues
- Reject face-swap intent
- Reject deception-oriented requests
- Keep negative constraints attached to every payload
